First Date Dec 20th, 2023

Opryland Hotel and Ice!

Our first official date as a couple was seriously one of the most fun days of my life. I remember being so excited to get to go to the Opry Hotel together and meet your parents (I was a little nervous about that though haha). It really was such a fun time, from going through Ice! together to walking around the hotel and eating dinner to ending the night with looking at all the lights, I couldn't have been any happier. I couldn't take my eyes off of you and I just felt so blessed to be there with you. Little did I know this would be the beginning of a tradition of ours that I look forward to every year and I can't wait to continue it as the years go on!

Snow Day Jan 16th, 2024

First Snow Day Fun!

Getting out of college for a week from snow in the first week was definitely very surprising but it made for such a fun time and experience. I remember us literally dying to see each other so much that you just decided to brave the winter storm and drive to my place to stay with me. You got there that night and we went out and walked around and played in the snow at my apartment complex. It was so much fun getting to experience a Snow Day with you. I remember just loving the way that the white snow made your eyes pop and thinking how beautiful you were. I must say, as cold as it was, it made me feel pretty warm to have you there with me.
